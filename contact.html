Mission Concepts & Partnerships

If you are reaching out about a mission concept, technology collaboration, or funding conversation, include a short note on the use case, timeline, and the type of partnership you have in mind.

Tip: If you are reaching out about a time-sensitive opportunity, please include your preferred timeline and any hard external deadlines in the email body.

Join Nebulous

Postdocs and collaborators are encouraged to have strong foundations in physics, computer science, or engineering — with relevant expertise in space sciences (heliophysics, astrophysics, planetary science, or Earth science), evolutionary computation and optimization, AI/ML, aerospace engineering, or science instrument design (broadly defined, not limited to radio or antennas). If your foundational expertise is not listed but you believe it would be valuable to Nebulous, we still encourage you to reach out.

If you bring curiosity and creativity — and you enjoy exploring new approaches to hard design problems — we’d be glad to hear from you.
