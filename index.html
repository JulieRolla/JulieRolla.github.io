AI-driven evolution of hardware

Nebulous is a research collaboration focused on using evolutionary algorithms and AI to design next-generation science hardware. By treating instrument concepts as evolving candidates, we rapidly explore large design spaces and converge on solutions that are high-performing, feasible to build, and mission-relevant.

Search, simulation, and trade studies

Pure optimization can fail when objectives are incomplete, constraints are complex, or the operating environment is difficult to model. Nebulous combines evolutionary algorithms, AI, and multi-objective optimization with science-grade Monte Carlo and digital-twin simulations, producing more robust solutions and design trade studies that engineers can act on.

From requirements to buildable concepts

Nebulous starts with requirements, constraints, and performance goals, then generates and evaluates candidate hardware designs through physics-based simulation. The result is more than an optimized geometry: it is a traceable trade study that helps teams understand performance, risk, and buildability before moving toward fabrication and testing.
